2D Rubik's Cube

Rules of the game

The goal of the game. A 4x4 playing field is presented in front of you. You need to collect your own color in each of the 4 squares. You can rotate 5 zones.

How to control the movement of squares? You can control by clicking the left mouse button on the corresponding zone. When you hover over an available area, you will notice that the area to click is highlighted. Keyboard control is also available, working keys: key 7, key 9, key 5, key 1, key 3.

Good luck and have a nice game.
